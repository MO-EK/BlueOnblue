Rules & Regulations for Displaying Items

  • Items must be in good condition/useable and thoroughly cleaned.
  • We accept only items that meet quality standards.
  • A 20% commission fee will be charged on items sold.
  • Items will be displayed for a maximum of 30 days.
  • Please provide accurate descriptions and pricing for all items.
  • Items must be dropped off during store hours.
  • blueOnblue reserves the right to refuse any item not meeting our criteria.
  • All items left after the display period will be donated or discarded.
